MOBILE OPERATING SYSTEM 16 Mar 2023

A mobile operating system (OS) is the software that runs on a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet. It is responsible for managing the device's hardware and software resources, providing a user interface for users to interact with the device, and running applications. There are several mobile operating systems available, including Android, iOS, and Windows.

BLOCKCHAIN 16 Jan 2023

Blockchain is a decentralized, digital ledger technology that enables secure and transparent transactions. It is essentially a database of records, or blocks, that are linked together using cryptography to form a chain. Each block contains a unique code, or hash, and information about a transaction, such as the sender, recipient, and timestamp.

MOBILE APPLICATIONS 29 Feb 2021

Mobile applications, or apps, are software programs that are designed to run on mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. They are developed for different operating systems such as Android, iOS, and Windows. Mobile apps can be downloaded from app stores, such as Google Play and the Apple App Store.

DESIGN THINKING 16 Jan 2023

Design thinking is a problem-solving approach that involves empathizing with users, defining the problem, ideating potential solutions, prototyping, and testing. It is a creative and iterative process that puts the user at the center of the design process, with the aim of creating solutions that meet their needs.
